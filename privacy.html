Privacy Notice

OtakuMart KE is a frontend-only demo marketplace created for a course capstone project. This site is designed with privacy and ethics in mind.

What data we collect

  • Registration form details (such as name and email) that you type in.
  • Essential cookie data (used only to identify a returning browser session).

How we use the data

  • To demonstrate frontend form validation.
  • To show a simple cookie-based user identifier.
  • To improve user experience.

Important note (Demo Project)

This website does NOT connect to a backend server or database. Your details are not stored permanently and are not shared with any third parties.

Kenya privacy & legal awareness

This project is developed with awareness of Kenya’s digital privacy and cybersecurity laws, including:

  • Kenya Data Protection Act (2019)
  • Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act (2018)
  • National ICT Policy (2019)
  • Guidelines from the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC)

These laws encourage fair, transparent, and lawful processing of personal data.

Your rights

Under the Data Protection Act (2019), you have rights such as:

  • Being informed about data collection
  • Accessing personal data
  • Correcting inaccurate data
  • Requesting deletion (where applicable)
